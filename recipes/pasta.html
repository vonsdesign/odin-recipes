Bologanise

Bolognaise

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive OIl
  • Beef Mince
  • Anchovie
  • Tomatoes

Cooking instructions

  1. Add Pasta to Boiling Water
  2. Heat Olive OIl
  3. Fry off mince
  4. Add tomatoes & Anchovies
  5. Serve pasta with sauce ontop, garnish with basil
