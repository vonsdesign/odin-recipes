Beef Roast

Beef roast

  • 5kg Beef
  • Oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • potatoes
  • Rosemary

Cooking instructions

  1. Heat oven 200c
  2. Oil, salt and pepper Beef
  3. Add to oven
  4. Peel potatoes, add to oven with rosemary
  5. Cook for 2 hours
  6. Serve hot
